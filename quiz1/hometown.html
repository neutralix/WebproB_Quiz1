Jakarta



Jakarta is the capital and largest city of Indonesia. The city had many names before such as Sunda Kelapa, Jayakarta, Batavia, Djakarta and lastly became Jakarta. Jakarta got its many names from before the Colonial era. Now, the official name used is Daerah Khusus Ibukota Jakarta, which literally means Jakarta Capital Special Region.

Jakarta consists of 5 administrative cities, each headed by a mayor. The administrative cities are Central Jakarta, West Jakarta, South Jakarta, East Jakarta, North Jakarta.

Jakarta's average weather temperature is quite high, around 30 degrees Celcius. It's peak can reach 34 degrees Celcius. It feels hot living in Jakarta. The air quality in this city is also considered bad. Combine it with the crowded traffic and you will get a bad experience visiting this city, but this city is really large and advanced. There are many big and tall building here.
